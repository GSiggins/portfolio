About Me

Born and raised in California I spent my childhood enjoying the California sun, swimming during the sweltering summer days, and riding my bike until the streetlights turned on. Fast forward to highschool, where a love for photography and graphic design was born, I found myself pursuing photography through college. A chance exposure to Emergency Medical Services ingited a fire for helping others that led down a path to paramedicine, working in 911, as well as providing medical services to local law enforcement agencies.
